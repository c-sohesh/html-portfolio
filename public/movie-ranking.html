The Best Animes According to Me

My top 3 animes of all-time.

One Piece

It's a anime about a pirate boy who wants to be The King of the Pirates. Adventerous, funny, motivational.

Death Note

It is a anime in which there is a book. The writer can write the person's name and cause of death. It was my first anime.

HxH

It's called Hunter X Hunter. I have not completed the whole anime yet but I like it so far.
